# Resources for Behavioral Cloning and Distributed Training

## Behavioral Cloning:

1. **Udacity Self-Driving Car Nanodegree - Behavioral Cloning Project:**
   - [Udacity Behavioral Cloning Project](https://github.com/udacity/CarND-Behavioral-Cloning-P3)
   - A project from Udacity's Self-Driving Car Nanodegree that provides hands-on experience with behavioral cloning.

2. **End-to-End Deep Learning for Self-Driving Cars (NVIDIA):**
   - [NVIDIA End-to-End Deep Learning](https://developer.nvidia.com/blog/deep-learning-self-driving-cars/)
   - NVIDIA's approach to end-to-end deep learning for self-driving cars, including behavioral cloning.

## Distributed Training:

3. **TensorFlow Distributed Training Guide:**
   - [TensorFlow Distributed Training Guide](https://www.tensorflow.org/guide/distributed_training)
   - Official guide from TensorFlow on distributed training, explaining different strategies and setups.

4. **PyTorch Distributed Training Tutorials:**
   - [PyTorch Distributed Training Tutorials](https://pytorch.org/tutorials/intermediate/ddp_tutorial.html)
   - Official PyTorch tutorials on distributed training, including DataParallel and DistributedDataParallel.

## Deep Learning Frameworks:

5. **TensorFlow Documentation:**
   - [TensorFlow Documentation](https://www.tensorflow.org/)
   - Comprehensive documentation for TensorFlow, including guides, tutorials, and API references.

6. **PyTorch Documentation:**
   - [PyTorch Documentation](https://pytorch.org/docs/stable/index.html)
   - Official documentation for PyTorch, covering installation, tutorials, and detailed API documentation.

## General Deep Learning:

7. **Deep Learning Specialization (Coursera - Andrew Ng):**
   - [Deep Learning Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning)
   - A popular deep learning specialization by Andrew Ng on Coursera, covering various aspects of deep learning.

8. **Dive into Deep Learning (D2L):**
   - [Dive into Deep Learning](https://d2l.ai/)
   - An interactive deep learning book with code, focusing on the fundamentals and practical aspects.

## Real-World Testing and Safety:

9. **CARLA Simulator:**
   - [CARLA Simulator](https://carla.org/)
   - An open-source simulator for autonomous driving research that can be used for testing and validation.

10. **Safety Considerations in Autonomous Vehicles (Waymo):**
    - [Waymo Safety Report](https://waymo.com/safety/)
    - Waymo's safety approach and considerations in autonomous driving.
